Overview of the ShopSense app

ShopSense is a location aware shopping companion. It connects your to-buy list with the places you pass in daily life.

The problem

Many people keep to-buy lists, but they often forget to check them when it matters. You might pass a shop that sells an item you need and only remember it later at home. This leads to missed tasks and extra trips. Most reminder apps work by time, not by location, so they cannot help at the right moment.

The ShopSense solution

ShopSense gives smart, location based reminders. You create a to-buy list, set your home location, and turn on Shopping Mode when heading out. The app quietly checks nearby stores that match your list and alerts you when you are close to a useful shop. You can control reminder frequency so notifications stay timely and manageable.

Main features

  • Create and manage a simple to-buy list.
  • Group items by category such as Bakery, Supermarket, or Pharmacy.
  • Set your home location as a safe starting point.
  • Turn on Shopping Mode to receive nearby store suggestions.

How it works in general

  1. Add items to your to-buy list with the right category.
  2. Set your home location inside the app.
  3. When you go out, enable Shopping Mode so ShopSense can remind you as you move near useful stores.
