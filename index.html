Remember what to buy at the right place and time.

Overview of the ShopSense app

ShopSense is a location aware shopping companion that connects your to-buy list with the places you pass in daily life.

The problem

Many people keep to-buy lists on their phones or in notebooks. Yet it is easy to forget to check the list when you pass a shop that sells something you need, which leads to missed tasks and extra trips.

Most reminder apps work on time only, not on place, so they cannot help at the right moment.

The ShopSense solution

ShopSense gives simple, location based shopping reminders. You create a to-buy list, set your home location, and turn on Shopping Mode when you head out.

The app quietly looks for nearby stores that match the categories on your list and alerts you when you are close to a useful shop.

Main features

  • Create and manage a simple to-buy list.
  • Set your home location as a starting point for reminders.
  • Turn on Shopping Mode to receive nearby store suggestions.

How it works in general

  1. Add items to your to-buy list with the right category.
  2. Set your home location inside the app.
  3. ShopSense can remind you as you move near useful stores.

Your to-buy list

Learn how to add items, change them, remove them, and mark them as done.

How to add an item to the list

  1. On the home screen tap the Add Item button at the bottom right.
  2. In the Add Item window type the item Name.
  3. Enter the Quantity and choose a Category such as Bakery or Pharmacy.
  4. Tap Confirm. The item appears in your list.

How to edit, delete, or check off an item

Edit an item

  1. Find the item you want to change in the list.
  2. Tap the pencil icon next to the item.
  3. Update the name, quantity, or category, then save.

Delete an item

  1. Tap the bin icon next to the item.
  2. Confirm the delete action.

Mark an item as done

  1. Tap the check box on the left side of the item.
  2. The item is marked as bought so you can see what you have already picked up.

Set your home location

ShopSense uses your home as the starting point for Shopping Mode. Once it is set, the app can see when you leave home and begin searching for useful stores nearby.

Why setting your home matters

Your home location tells ShopSense when you have gone out for the day. When you move outside this boundary, the app can start watching for shops that match your to-buy list.

How to set your home location

  1. Open the app and tap the Update Home button on the home screen.
  2. Choose Use current location or tap Pick on map.
  3. If you pick on the map, move the pin if needed and set the Radius.
  4. Tap Save to store your home area.
  5. If asked, allow ShopSense to use your location while the app is running.

Tip. You can change your home at any time using the same Update Home button.

Shopping mode

Shopping Mode turns your to-buy list into gentle location based reminders while you move around.

How to turn on Shopping Mode

Method 1. From the home screen

  1. Make sure your to-buy list has a few items.
  2. On the home screen, tap the Shopping Mode switch.
  3. The app may ask you to allow notifications. Choose Allow.
  4. If you see a message asking for location in the background, follow the steps and set location to Allow all the time.
  5. When Shopping Mode is active, you see a status notification with a Snooze option. Snooze pauses Shopping Mode for ten minutes without turning it off completely.

Method 2. From a reminder notification

  1. When you move outside your home area, ShopSense can send a notification that asks Activate Shopping Mode?
  2. Tap Yes to start Shopping Mode without opening the app.

Tip. You can turn Shopping Mode off at any time by switching it off on the home screen.

What happens when Shopping Mode is on

While Shopping Mode is active, ShopSense runs a light foreground service that checks your location at intervals and compares it with the categories in your to-buy list.

Behind the scenes

  1. The app reads your to-buy items and their categories such as Bakery or Pharmacy.
  2. As you move, it looks for nearby stores that match those categories.
  3. When a matching store is close enough, you receive a notification that names the place and lists what you can buy there.
  4. If you tap Show direction, Google Maps opens with directions to that store.

Things to keep in mind

  • It works best when each item in your list has the correct category.
  • You can snooze notifications or switch Shopping Mode off whenever you are done shopping.
