ShopSense Guide

Welcome to ShopSense

This guide shows you how to create your shopping list and use Shopping Mode step by step.

Add an item to your list

  1. Tap the Add Item button at the bottom right.
  2. Type the item Name, Quantity, and select a Category.
  3. Tap Confirm to save.
  4. You will see “Item added successfully” at the bottom of the screen.
